# Clarion — Design Rationale

## Identity

A modern heraldic insignia emerging from noise and darkness, symbolizing important information becoming clear, visible, and worthy of attention.

## Logo concept

Clarion's logo is inspired by the idea of a modern herald: something that brings important information into focus.

We deliberately moved away from literal herald imagery such as horns, trumpets, scrolls, banners, or messengers. While historically accurate, those symbols felt either too medieval, too decorative, or too narrowly tied to the etymology of the word clarion rather than the purpose of the product.

Instead, we focused on the core concept behind both the name and the app: important signals emerging from noise and obscurity. Clarion doesn't create information; it takes messages from tools, scripts, and automation workflows and surfaces them clearly through native macOS notifications.

## Mark

The final mark is a custom herald-inspired insignia rendered in warm gold against a dark background. The insignia represents significance, trust, and announcement, while the surrounding noise and fragmented darkness symbolize the clutter and chaos from which important information emerges.

The result is intentionally symbolic rather than literal: a recognizable emblem that evokes clarity, importance, arrival, and good news, while remaining modern, scalable, and suitable for a macOS utility rather than a historical or fantasy-themed product.
